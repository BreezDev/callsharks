A journey that started with a belief in lead quality

CallSharks began with one goal: give mortgage teams a reliable way to reach high-intent borrowers without wasting time on low-quality data. We built our subscriptions around verified intent, clear timelines, and fast delivery.

Today, we support lenders, brokers, and call centers nationwide with mortgage leads that are ready for outreach. As we grow, we’re expanding into insurance and other lead categories so your pipeline can grow with us.
